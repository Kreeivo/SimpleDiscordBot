/info -- Displays information about the current song
/pause -- Pauses the current song playing
/play song [URL] -- Plays a song (Must be a valid youtube URL)
/play search [KEYWORDS] -- Plays a song based off the keywords
/queue -- Displays the queue
/quit -- The bot will leave the voice channel
/resume -- resumes the current song playing
/shuffle -- shuffles the current playlist
/skip -- skips to the next song in the playlist
/skipto -- skips to a chosen song within the current playlist
